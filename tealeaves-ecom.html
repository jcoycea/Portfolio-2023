TEALEAVES: E-Comm Revamp A redesign of the Shopify e-commerce store to create a seamless experience grounded in brand storytelling, visual elegance, and thoughtful UX.

Overview TEALEAVES stands as one of the last true in-house tea blenders. With unmatched craftsmanship, exceptional quality and flavor, they redefine luxury through a commitment to innovation and biodiversity-positive design—inviting consumers to indulge in a refined experience that honors both elegance and the planet.

To elevate this experience online, the Design Team led a comprehensive revamp of the Shopify ecommerce store. The goal was to create a seamless, immersive digital journey that reflects TEALEAVES’ luxurious, sustainability-focused ethos. In close collaboration with the Marketing team, we streamlined content by reducing excess copy and visual noise, brought consistency to product detail pages and key shopping pathways, and used interactive storytelling and rich visuals to encourage deeper exploration. The result is a cohesive, elevated experience that showcases the brand’s prestige and the artistry behind every blend.
