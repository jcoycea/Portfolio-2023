Athiana Acres A regenerative, organic farm located in the heart of Richmond, British Columbia, growing a diverse range of vibrant vegetables, fruits, herbs and flowers.

Overview Launched in Fall 2022, with a vision to transform the land into a thriving regenerative ecosystem, Athiana Acres is working to create a space that is not only incredibly productive in growing high-quality, nourishing foods, but also a place filled with beautiful landscapes, intentionally designed to work in harmony with nature.

I was brought on as a freelance designer by the Managing Director, Simran, to work on expanding the Athiana Acres Shopify site and creating new webpages. I also assisted with additional graphic design projects, working on updating the Introduction Deck, designing brochures for the Floral and Farm offerings, and billboard signage.
