Fantastic Mushroom Tea A limited edition blend created in partnership with Fantastic Fungi, sip and cultivate deeper connections with nature to inspire and protect the planet.⁠

OverviewAn exclusive collaboration between mushroom experts and tea experts. The TEALEAVES Design team was tasked with creating the packaging for a custom mushroom tea blend with Fantastic Fungi. The product is featured in Fantastic Fungi’s Forager Box, a curated box of products meant to integrate mushrooms into one’s daily rituals.

GraphicsThe Design team worked with the Fantastic Fungi team to create this collaboration product, and they graciously shared with us a library of photography assets to use on the packaging. We incorporated the incredible photography from Louie Schwartzberg, director of the critically acclaimed documentary, on the outside and inside of the box to showcase the world of mushrooms.

PackagingInspired by decadent chocolate packaging, we created a new slim box to hold the pouch of tea inside, and as a result, introduced a new product format into the TEALEAVES packaging ecosystem. The large folded flaps allow for variable typography on the outside and inside to educate on the collaboration and the tea itself. The diecut shape of the flaps with the scalloped edges is inspired by camellia sinensis flower petals, and act as our reference to the tea plant.

Design DecisionsThe box has a “surprise and delight” element with the contrast of elegant dark exterior with a brightly coloured interior. The dark outer portrays the mushroom tea in luxurious manner, while the inside orange colours are aligned with the accent colours from the Fantastic Fungi branding; it could also be said that the orange colour reflects the warmth and spicy notes of the mushroom tea ingredients. After someone opens the box, the tea in the clear pouch is meant to be the focal point of the box, where a person’s eyes are drawn before they take in the rest of the visuals.

Final ProductFor the final product, the Fantastic Mushroom Tea’s visual style employs an elevated and modern design. The packaging itself is sustainable, comprised of a recyclable box and a compostable clear pouch. We hope people feel luxurious when they open the packaging and incorporate the tea into their daily wellness ritual.

TakeawayThe team was happy to collaborate with Fantastic Fungi as we were fascinated and greatly inspired by the documentary and aligned with their ethos and love of nature.
